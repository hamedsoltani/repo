# Privacy Policy autopush app respects your privacy. This app collects and stores minimal data required to function properly. 1. **Data Collected** - Access tokens from social media platforms for the purpose of uploading videos. - Optional: local file paths of videos or images you provide. 2. **Data Usage** - Tokens are used only to upload content to your authorized accounts. - No data is shared with third parties. 3. **Data Storage** - All data is stored locally on your computer. - No information is sent to external servers. 4. **Security** - Access tokens are stored securely in local configuration files. - You are responsible for securing your device. By using this app, you consent to this privacy policy.
