auto_push

A tool to upload and manage your social media videos securely using official APIs.

About

auto_push is designed to help users upload videos to platforms like TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. Users authorize the app via official APIs, and videos are uploaded only to accounts they control.

Features

  • Upload videos to your own social media accounts
  • Manage video content with privacy and security
  • Supports TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook
